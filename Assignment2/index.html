The Graduate

The Graduate is a classic movie about a recent college graduate that doesn't know what to do after college. The main character is seduced into having an affair with a friend of his parents. After the affair, he falls in love with the daughter of his parents' friend.

The Graduate is my favorite movie because it was made when acting was relatively new, so I can't help but laugh at some of the scenes. The plot is simple and effective. Also, the soundtrack for the movie is done by Simon and Garfunkel.

Characters:

  • Ben Braddock
  • Elaine Robinson
  • Mr. and Mrs. Robinson
  • Mr. and Mrs. Braddock
  • Carl Smith

Quote from Movie:

Benjamin: Oh, she doesn't. To be perfectly honest, she doesn't like me.
